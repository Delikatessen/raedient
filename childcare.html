Childcare Cleaning Done Right

Clean, healthy spaces for children and staff

Childcare centers need special attention when it comes to cleaning. Kids are more vulnerable to germs and harsh cleaning chemicals, so we use safe, effective products and methods to create healthy environments.

From toys and play areas to classrooms and restrooms, we keep your childcare center clean without using harsh chemicals that might harm the children in your care.

Why Specialized Childcare Cleaning Matters

Creating safer spaces for kids

Germ Control

We reduce illness spread with thorough disinfection of high-touch surfaces that meets childcare regulations.

Child Safety

We use products that clean effectively without exposing children to harsh chemicals or fumes.

Parent Confidence

A visibly clean center shows parents you care about their children's wellbeing and health.

Meeting Standards

Our cleaning helps you meet health department and childcare licensing requirements.

Play Areas and Toys

Play areas and toys need special attention. They're touched constantly and often end up in mouths, making them perfect germ transfer points.

We clean and sanitize play equipment, toys, and surfaces using child-safe methods that remove germs without leaving harmful residues.

  • Toy cleaning and sanitizing
  • Play structure disinfection
  • Floor cleaning in play zones
  • Soft surfaces like mats and rugs

Classrooms and Learning Spaces

Classrooms need to be clean, germ-free environments where kids can learn and grow. We pay special attention to desks, tables, and frequently touched items.

Our cleaning includes proper disinfection of learning materials, shared supplies, and educational toys that get handled throughout the day.

  • Desk and table sanitizing
  • Floor cleaning and spot treatment
  • High-touch surface disinfection
  • Cleaning of learning materials and supplies

Restrooms and Changing Areas

Childcare restrooms need extra attention. We thoroughly clean and sanitize these spaces to prevent illness spread and maintain proper hygiene.

Our team uses effective cleaning methods for child-sized fixtures and changing areas, focusing on both cleanliness and safety.

  • Thorough toilet and sink cleaning
  • Diaper changing station sanitizing
  • Floor disinfection
  • Touchpoint cleaning (handles, faucets, etc.)
