Entrances and Windows

First impressions count. Your store's entrance and windows are the first things customers see, and they set expectations for their shopping experience.

We keep these high-visibility areas spotless, ensuring smudge-free glass, clean floors, and tidy entryways that welcome customers into your space.

  • Window and glass door cleaning
  • Entryway floor care
  • Display window dusting and cleaning
  • Entry mat cleaning

Shopping Areas and Displays

The main shopping floor needs consistent attention to stay clean and presentable throughout business hours. Dust and fingerprints can quickly accumulate on shelves, displays, and merchandise.

We clean these areas carefully, working around products and displays to ensure your merchandise looks its best and remains undisturbed.

  • Floor cleaning suited to your flooring type
  • Careful dusting of shelves and displays
  • Fingerprint removal from glass cases
  • Trash management throughout the space

Fitting Rooms and Customer Areas

Fitting rooms can make or break a sale. These private spaces need special attention to stay clean, odor-free, and presentable for each customer.

We clean mirrors, benches, hooks, and floors thoroughly, ensuring these critical conversion areas remain inviting and comfortable for shoppers.

  • Mirror cleaning and streak removal
  • Bench and seating sanitizing
  • Hook and fixture cleaning
  • Floor cleaning and deodorizing
